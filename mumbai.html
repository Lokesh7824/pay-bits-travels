About Mumbai

Mumbai, the city of dreams, is a bustling metropolis known for its vibrant culture, historic landmarks, and stunning coastline. From the iconic Gateway of India to the bustling markets, Mumbai offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Mumbai is from **November to February**, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.
