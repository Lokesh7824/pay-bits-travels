Explore Lonavala - The Jewel of the Western Ghats

About Lonavala

Lonavala, nestled in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra, is a picturesque hill station known for its lush green valleys, serene lakes, and stunning waterfalls. It's a perfect getaway for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those seeking a peaceful retreat.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Lonavala is **October to May**, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

Top Attractions

  • Tiger's Leap: A cliff-top with a breathtaking view of the valley.
