About Kashmir

Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, serene lakes, and lush valleys. From the iconic Dal Lake to the snow-capped peaks of Gulmarg, Kashmir offers a tranquil escape into nature's beauty.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kashmir is from **March to October**, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing, trekking, and enjoying the natural beauty.
