Discover Goa

About Goa

Goa, India's beach paradise, is known for its stunning coastline, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. From the golden sands of Calangute Beach to the serene Dudhsagar Falls, Goa offers a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Goa is from **November to February**, when the weather is cool and perfect for beach activities and sightseeing.
